`@zkscatter/sdk` is the TypeScript toolkit for talking to every part of
the zkScatter protocol — contracts, ZK proofs, relayer network, shared
orderbook, and on-device note storage. **One package, one mental model**,
ship a wallet on web, mobile, or Node.

The same SDK powers the four reference apps shipped by Tokamak —
[Pro](https://pro.zkscatter.xyz), [Pay](https://pay.zkscatter.xyz),
[Drop](https://drop.zkscatter.xyz), and Mobile. Anything those apps do,
your app can do, with the same primitives.

## How a trade flows

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deposit">
    The user signs a deposit ZK proof on-device. `CommitmentPool.deposit`
    inserts a Poseidon commitment hiding the owner secret, token, and amount.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    The user generates a half-proof of their order: an EdDSA signature
    over `(side, amounts, expiry, relayer)`, plus a Groth16 proof binding
    it to a leaf in the commitment tree.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Match">
    A relayer matches the order against the shared orderbook and produces
    the counterparty's half-proof. Both halves go on-chain through
    `PrivateSettlement.settleAuth`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim">
    The buyer's payout arrives at a stealth address as a new commitment.
    They claim later — any time, gaslessly — by proving knowledge of the
    spending key.
  </Step>
</Steps>
